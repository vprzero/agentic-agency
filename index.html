The best agencies in this industry are burning out their best people. Endless RFP cycles. Budget spreadsheets built from scratch. Permit hunts that take weeks. Decks that start over every pitch. The work that actually matters — the creative thinking, the cultural instinct, the fan connection — gets squeezed into whatever hours are left.

We built Dime Shock's AI agent system to fix that. Not to replace human creativity — to protect it.

New Way

Most permit delays aren't about process — they're about finding the right human and speaking their language. The agent knows both. Your team approves the email before it goes. But the research, the contact, and the framing are handled in an hour instead of two weeks. Permits become a system, not a prayer.
